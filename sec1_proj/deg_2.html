The Second Degree

shield


This spell has no incantation. Instead, it requires your creativity to conjure a shield that will protect you from an oncoming attack. Keep in mind the tendencies, natural strengths, and weaknesses of your element.
push
"Baka"

Both hands shoves out in front and away from your body. Palms are open and face the target.
Builds on the principles of Telekinesis, but requires more energy and the trigger word for its greater impact.
disarm
"Disablo"

Use your dominant hand to yank at the air towards yourself.
Has same foundations as Telekinesis, except it requires greater finesse, as you must exert your will not just on the item, but to a degree that overpowers your opponent's strength of grasp on their weapon.
slam
"Grau"

Your dominant hand makes a throwing motion, from above the shoulder and down to waist-level.
Focus on your palm. See your gesture interrupt the natural flow of air, understand that the fundamental of this spell has you crack the air.
